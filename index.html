About Me

I'm a Developer

I Develop Websites and Applications

Hello! I’m Lucas, a passionate front-end developer and tech enthusiast. I love building elegant, user-friendly interfaces and experimenting with the latest web technologies. With strong expertise in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and frameworks like React, I bring ideas to life on the web.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked on various personal and freelance projects, focusing on creating responsive, accessible, and pixel-perfect designs.

Currently, I’m expanding my skillset with backend technologies like Node.js and Python to become a full-stack developer. I enjoy tackling challenges that push me to grow and learn new things. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring the latest tech trends, playing guitar, or hiking with friends.

I am excited to connect with like-minded individuals and explore new opportunities. Whether you’re interested in collaborating on a project or simply want to chat about the latest trends in web development, feel free to reach out!
